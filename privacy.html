Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information across our services.

Our Commitment to Privacy

LeafSpark Garden Pruning is committed to maintaining the confidentiality and security of your personal information while providing a safe and trustworthy online experience. Our privacy practices uphold the highest standards of data protection and transparency.

1. Types of Information We Collect

We collect a broad range of information to provide our services efficiently and enhance your experience, including but not limited to:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, postal address, email address, phone number, and billing details.
  • Payment & Transaction Data: Credit/debit card information processed securely by trusted third parties.
  • Technical Device Information: IP address, device type, operating system, and browser data to improve functionality.
  • Online Activity Data: Browsing history, search queries, clicks, and purchases related to our website.
  • Communications: Correspondence and customer support interactions.

2. Use of Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies and similar technologies are used for various purposes such as remembering preferences, analyzing user behavior, providing personalized content, and targeted advertising. Our cookies include:

  • Strictly necessary cookies: Essential for site functionality and security.
  • Performance cookies: Help understand user engagement and site performance.
  • Functionality cookies: Remembering your preferences and previous actions.
  • Advertising cookies: Serve relevant ads and measure their effectiveness.

You can configure your browser to accept or reject cookies; however, disabling cookies may limit some features.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information enables us to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our service offerings.
  • Manage customer accounts and process payments securely.
  • Communicate updates, promotions, and service details.
  • Personalize website content and recommendations.
  • Fulfill legal and regulatory requirements.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure the security of our services.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Trusted service providers performing functions on our behalf (e.g., payment processors, mailing services, analytics providers).
  • Law enforcement or regulators as required by law.
  • Business partners and affiliates for joint services, with your consent.

All third parties are obligated to protect your information and are prohibited from using it for other purposes.

5. Data Security and Protection

Our security measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL protocols.
  • Secure storage with restricted access.
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing.
  • Employee training on data protection practices.
  • Incident response plans for promptly addressing any security breach.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the rights to:

  • Access your data and obtain a copy.
  • Request corrections or updates to your information.
  • Withdraw consent or object to processing.
  • Request deletion of your information where applicable.
  • Limit use of your data for direct marketing.
  • Receive data portability where applicable.
  • Lodge complaints with data protection authorities.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at privacy@leafspark.com.

7. Retention of Your Data

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above and to comply with legal, tax, and accounting requirements. After this, we securely delete or anonymize your information.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our website and services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware of such data, we will promptly delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the country where we operate, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries that may have different data protection laws. We use appropriate safeguards to protect your data in these cases.

10. Links to Other Websites

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in legal requirements or our practices. Updates will be posted here, and we encourage you to review this page periodically.
