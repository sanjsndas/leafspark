Introduction

This detailed Cookies Policy explains the types of cookies used by LeafSpark Garden Pruning ("we", "us", "our") on our website, how they impact your experience, and your rights and choices about them. This policy spans extensive aspects of cookies from technical definitions to compliance with global privacy laws.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files set by websites on your browser or device when you visit. These files store information such as login tokens, user preferences, and analytical data to facilitate a seamless and personalized browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential (Strictly Necessary) Cookies

These cookies are vital for core website functions like security, privacy preferences, or session management. They ensure our site operates correctly and without them, certain services cannot function.

Performance and Analytical Cookies

These collect aggregated, anonymous data about visitor behavior like page visits, duration, and interaction patterns. We use this to analyze traffic trends and improve our website's performance.

Functional Cookies

Functional cookies enable enhanced features such as remembering your language preference or login status, pushing a more interactive and customized user experience.

Targeting and Advertising Cookies

Used to deliver relevant advertisements and offers tailored to your interests. These cookies track browsing habits and help measure campaign effectiveness.

Social Media Cookies

Cookies from social media platforms facilitate content sharing and engagement with our social channels but are governed by their policies.

How We Use Cookies

  • To maintain secure login and shopping sessions
  • To analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • To personalize content and offers
  • To provide social media functionalities
  • To measure effectiveness of advertising campaigns

Third-Party Cookies

We partner with several third-party providers for analytics and advertising that may place cookies on your browser. These providers operate independently and their use of cookies is subject to their own privacy policies.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

We provide you with full control over your cookie preferences. On your first visit, a cookie consent banner appears enabling you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can modify or withdraw your consent at any time through your browser settings or by revisiting the banner.

How to Manage Cookies via Browser

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Manage and delete cookies and site data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data

Clearing Cookies

You can clear cookies at any time from your browser. Note that doing this may log you out of websites and reset preferences.

Cookie Duration

Cookies may be session-based (deleted when browser closes) or persistent (stored for longer periods). The lifespan varies depending on the cookie purpose.

Legal Compliance

Our cookie practices comply with regulations such as GDPR, CCPA, and ePrivacy Directive to protect your rights and give you transparency.

Impact of Declining Cookies

Declining non-essential cookies may limit access to some features, reduce personalization, or impact site functionality and services.

Use of Do Not Track (DNT) Signals

Currently, we do not respond directly to browser Do Not Track signals but we honor your cookie consent preferences through our banner.

Security and Protection of Cookie Data

Data collected through cookies is processed securely using encryption, access controls, and other safeguards to prevent unauthorized use.
